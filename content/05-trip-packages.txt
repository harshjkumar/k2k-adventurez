TRIP 1: 10 Days | Srinagar – Leh Ladakh – Manali
================================================================================

Slug: 10-days-srinagar-leh-ladakh-manali
Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights
Difficulty: Advanced
Season: May – Sep
Group Size: 10-20
Max Altitude: 18,380 ft (Khardung La)

Route: Srinagar → Sonamarg → Zoji La → Drass → Kargil → Lamayuru → Leh → Khardung La → Nubra Valley → Pangong Tso → Leh → Sarchu → Baralacha La → Manali
Tagline: The ultimate cross-Himalayan expedition from Srinagar to Manali via Leh, covering every iconic pass, lake and valley of Ladakh.
Terrain: High Passes, Valleys, Lakes, Deserts
Best For: Adventure Seekers, Long Riders
Rating: 4.8/5 (210 reviews)

Highlights: Zoji La Pass, Kargil War Memorial, Lamayuru Moonland, Khardung La, Nubra Valley, Pangong Tso, Gata Loops, Baralacha La, Atal Tunnel

ITINERARY:

Day 1: Srinagar – Arrival
All participants are required to arrive in Srinagar today. The day is at leisure to relax and explore the city. Enjoy a shikara ride on Dal Lake and visit the famous floating market. In the evening, your Road Captain will conduct a detailed briefing about the terrain and the journey, followed by bike allotment. Enjoy dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.
Overnight: Hotel in Srinagar

Day 2: Srinagar – Zojila pass – Kargil (200 km)
Srinagar – Sonamarg – Zojila Pass - Gumri Post – Drass – Kargil War Memorial- Kargil
Wake up early and start the journey after breakfast. Ride through the beautiful valley of Sonamarg and cross the famous Zoji La Pass, entering the rugged landscapes of Ladakh. Continue through Drass, known as the second coldest inhabited place in the world, and visit the Kargil War Memorial to pay tribute to the heroes of the Kargil War. Reach Kargil by evening for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.
Overnight: Hotel in Kargil

Day 3: Kargil – Leh (211 km)
Kargil – Mulbek – Namik La – Fotu La – Lamayuru – Khalsi – Nimmu – Leh
After breakfast, start your ride towards Leh along a scenic route following the river. En route, visit the famous Lamayuru Moonland and cross the high passes Namika La and Fotu La. Stop at the Indus–Zanskar Confluence (Sangam), then continue past Magnetic Hill and Gurudwara Pathar Sahib. You will also pass the Hall of Fame Museum. Reach Leh by evening for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.
Overnight: Hotel in Leh

Day 4: Leh (Local Sight Seeing)
Wake up at leisure and enjoy breakfast at the hotel. Today is a relaxed day to explore the beautiful city of Leh at your own pace. Visit popular attractions like the Hall of Fame Museum, Shanti Stupa, Thiksey Monastery, Alchi Monastery, Likir Monastery, and the historic Leh Palace. You can also explore the local Tibetan market and city markets. Evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.
Overnight: Hotel in Leh

Day 5: Leh – Khardung-La – Nubra Valley (150 km, 18,380 ft)
Leh – South Pullu – Khardung La – North Pullu – Khardung Village – Khalsar Village – Diskit Monastery – Hunder (Nubra Valley).
Start early after breakfast and ride to Khardung La Pass (18,380 ft), one of the highest motorable roads in the world. Continue the journey into the beautiful Nubra Valley, once part of the historic Silk Route. Visit the famous Diskit Monastery and reach the sand dunes of Hunder. Enjoy the scenic cold desert and optional camel rides on the unique double-humped Bactrian camels. Capture the stunning sunset and spend the night at Hunder.
Overnight: Camp in Nubra

Day 6: Nubra Valley – Pangong Tso (165 km)
Hunder – Agham – Shyok – Durbuk – Tangtse – Spangmik (Pangong Tso).
After breakfast, begin the adventurous ride toward Pangong Lake via the scenic Agham–Shyok route, a less-traveled but thrilling path that truly tests your riding skills. Reach the stunning Pangong Lake by afternoon and check into camps near the lake. Spend the evening enjoying the mesmerizing views of the lake and surrounding mountains. Dinner and overnight stay at the camp near Pangong.
Overnight: Camp at Pangong

Day 7: Pangong Tso – Leh (140 km)
Spangmik (Pangong Tso) – Tangtse – Chang La – Sakti – Karu – Leh
After breakfast, visit the famous 3 Idiots point at Pangong Lake and enjoy a photo session by the stunning lake. Later begin the journey back to Leh, crossing Chang La Pass, one of the highest motorable passes in the world. Halt for tea en route and continue the scenic ride. Reach Leh by evening for dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.
Overnight: Hotel in Leh

Day 8: Leh – Sarchu
Leh – Karu – Upsi – Moore Plains – Pang – Lachung La Pass – Nakee La Pass – Gata Loops – Sarchu
Start early from Leh after breakfast and drive along the scenic Leh–Manali Highway. Pass through Karu and Upshi, then cross the vast More Plains and Pang. Continue over Lachulung La Pass and Nakee La Pass, and drive through the famous Gata Loops (21 hairpin bends). Reach Sarchu by evening for dinner and overnight stay in camps.
Overnight: Camp in Sarchu

Day 9: Sarchu – Manali (225 km)
Sarchu – Killing Sarai – Baralacha La – Zing Zing Bar – Darcha – Jispa – Keylong – Tandi – Atal Tunnel – Manali
Start early from Sarchu as today's journey is one of the most adventurous drives of the trip. After breakfast, begin your drive towards Manali while crossing the spectacular Baralacha La Pass (16,040 ft). Along the way, enjoy views of the stunning Suraj Tal, a beautiful high-altitude lake located just below the pass. Continue your journey through the rugged landscapes of the Himalayas, passing scenic valleys and remote mountain roads. Later, drive through the famous Atal Tunnel, one of the longest highway tunnels in the world at high altitude, which connects the Lahaul Valley with Manali. By evening, arrive in Manali. Check in to the hotel and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay in Manali.
Overnight: Hotel in Manali

Day 10: Manali – Departure
Wake up, enjoy breakfast, and proceed to your onward journey. The expedition concludes in Manali with beautiful memories to cherish forever.
