--- FAQ SECTION ---

Q: What's included in the trip price?
A: Every trip includes a well-maintained Royal Enfield motorcycle, riding gear (helmet, jacket, gloves), all accommodations (mix of hotels, homestays, and camping), meals as specified, backup vehicle with mechanic, first-aid kit, ride captain, and a photography package.

Q: Do I need my own motorcycle?
A: No! We provide fully serviced Royal Enfield motorcycles. However, if you prefer riding your own bike, we offer 'Bring Your Own Bike' packages at a reduced price. Your bike must pass our safety inspection before the trip.

Q: What's the fitness level required?
A: It depends on the trip. Beginner trips (Rajasthan) require basic fitness. Moderate trips (Manali, Meghalaya) need moderate endurance. Advanced/Expert trips (Spiti, Ladakh) require good physical fitness as you'll ride at extreme altitudes. We send a fitness preparation guide 3 weeks before departure.

Q: Is it safe for solo travelers?
A: Absolutely! Over 40% of our riders are solo travelers. You'll be part of a group of 6-15 riders with experienced ride captains, a backup vehicle, and 24/7 support. Many of our solo riders end up making lifelong friends on the road.

Q: What happens if my bike breaks down?
A: Our backup vehicle follows the group at all times with a certified mechanic and spare parts. We carry common spares for all models. In rare cases of major breakdown, we swap your bike immediately. Your ride doesn't stop.

Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Yes. Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before departure. 50% refund for 15-29 days. No refund within 14 days. Trip date transfers are free up to 15 days before departure. We also offer trip insurance through our partner.

Q: What about accommodation quality?
A: We use hand-picked hotels (3-star minimum), authentic homestays, and premium camping setups. All accommodations are vetted for cleanliness, safety, and experience quality. In remote areas, we set up dome tents with sleeping bags and mattresses.

Q: Do you offer customized/private trips?
A: Yes! We offer private group packages for 4+ riders with customizable routes, dates, and accommodation preferences. Corporate team-building expeditions are also available. Contact us with your requirements for a tailored quote.
